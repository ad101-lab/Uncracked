New life for old devices

What We Do

We collect broken and unwanted devices, and give them new lives, by fixing them up, and sometimes reselling them. We accept donations of old phones, tablets, laptops, and other devices, no matter how broken they might be. We take them apart, see if we can fix them, and if all else fails, we use them for replacement parts in other devices.

About Us

We are three teens who aspire to be engineers, and this is our first business. We have fixed a few phones, but are still learning a lot, and by giving us your broken and unwanted devices, you are helping us learn.
